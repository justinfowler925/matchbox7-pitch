The Market — The Side-Effect Economy Is Unserved

The Unsolved Gap

GLP-1 drugs work — but 50–74% of users suffer side effects severe enough that 30%+ discontinue. No mainstream brand systematically addresses:

  • Constipation — slowed GI motility
  • Dehydration — reduced fluid intake
  • Muscle wasting — up to 50% of weight lost is lean mass
  • Nutrient malabsorption — delayed gastric emptying

The Science Underneath

Water-Soluble Monch Fibre

Monch is a proprietary water-soluble fibre that achieves ~40% gut absorption vs. ~12% for standard insoluble fibre. It is fermented by colonic microbiota into short-chain fatty acids (acetate, propionate, butyrate) at >95% efficiency, modulating the microbiome and stimulating endogenous GLP-1 production. Its solubility makes it universally incorporable into bars, drinks, powders, and baked goods — opening a B2B ingredient-licensing channel beyond our own product line.
