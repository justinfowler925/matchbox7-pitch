The Science

Water-Soluble Monch Fiber:
3.3x Higher Bioavailability

Monch is a proprietary water-soluble fiber that achieves approximately 40% absorption in the gut after ingestion — compared to ~12% for standard insoluble fiber supplements. This is possible because water-soluble fiber is readily fermented by colonic microbiota into short-chain fatty acids (SCFAs), which are absorbed at >95% efficiency in the colon.

🔬

Fermentation Pathway

Water-soluble fiber is readily fermented by gut microbiota into SCFAs (acetate, propionate, butyrate) at 300 mmol/day. Only 10 mmol/day excreted. >95% of SCFAs absorbed in the colon. (Topping & Clifton, Physiological Reviews)

💧

Water Solubility Advantage

Unlike insoluble fiber that passes through as bulk, Monch dissolves completely — enabling incorporation into bars, electrolyte packets, and functional beverages while maintaining full bioactive potential. Gel-forming action slows gastric emptying, reducing GLP-1 nausea.

🧬

Universal Incorporation

Water solubility means Monch can be incorporated into virtually any consumer food product — bars, drinks, powders, baked goods. This creates a B2B ingredient licensing opportunity beyond our own product line.
